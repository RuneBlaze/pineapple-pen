Hidden in a mythical corner of the world, Auroravale stands—a city where dreams and reality converge. Here, the celestial glow of auroras inspires human creations, reflected in crystal towers that capture daylight and illuminate nights. Tradition marries innovation in its cityscape, where old wooden homes nestle beside cutting-edge labs. Shrouded in secrecy, Auroravale is a nexus of knowledge and enigma. Come spring, cherry blossoms and neon lights dance together, symbolizing a harmony of nature and technology. A city off the map, Auroravale is where ancient lore and new discoveries weave a unique cultural tapestry—a living dream blending the wisdom of the past with the possibilities of the future.
